Our Mission

Our mission is to raise the interest of students in the fields of science and technology as they learn to solve problems in innovative ways.

We get to know each other, learn from each other and have lots of fun while striving towards a common goal.

As a team, we look forward to participating with "Gracious Professionalism" in FIRST's 2011 "Coopertition".

What is FIRST?

FIRST stands for "For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology" and was founded by inventor Dean Kamen. The FIRST Robotic Teams have to solve an engineering problem. Different people from all over the world are accepting the challenge to design a robot.
